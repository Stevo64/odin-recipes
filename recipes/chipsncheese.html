Chips n Cheese

Cheeseyy Cheezay chips

The pinnacle of your saturday night out, the cheesy chips to prevent hangovers

Ingredients

  • CHIPS
  • CHEESE
  • Copious amounts of cheese
  • tasty chips
  • Sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Place chips into air fryer
  2. remove and place into box
  3. Add cheese to chips and leave for 2-3min
  4. Add sauce/salt/vinegar to own desire
  5. Consume
