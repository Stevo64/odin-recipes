Hoagie Wrap

The amazing hoagie wrap

The Hoagie wrap is an elite glasgow dish which is most highly sought after on a sunday evening

Ingredients

  • Donner meat
  • Soft flour wraps
  • Copious amounts of cheese
  • tasty chips
  • Garlic mayo
  • Chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Place contents into wrap in any order
  2. Roll together with as many wraps as desired
  3. Use sauces to mend wraps together
  4. Place contents into air fryer (20MIN)
  5. Remove, slice and consume
